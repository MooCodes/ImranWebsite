Imran Baig, CPA

Imran Baig, CPA

I began my career in the nonprofit world straight out of college, starting as a staff accountant while earning my Certified Professional Accountant (CPA) credential. I worked my way up to accounting manager, then financial analyst, and, finally, interim controller at Lutheran Social Services, where I spent 11 years. I enjoy the detail work of accounting along with the mentoring aspect. As a self-proclaimed numbers nerd, I found that I wanted to share my skills and expertise to support and guide others in their businesses.
