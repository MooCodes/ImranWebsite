Expert Accounting Services You Can Count On

Hi. I'm Imran Baig, CPA, and I am here to provide you with reliable, personalized accounting services tailored to your unique needs. With a commitment to accuracy and a passion for helping clients navigate their financial landscapes, I offer expertise in tax preparation, financial planning, and more.

My goal is to simplify the complexities of accounting for you, so you can focus on what truly matters. Whether you're an individual seeking peace of mind with your finances or a business looking for strategic advice, I'm here to support you every step of the way.

Bookkeeping

High End Bookkeeping

My services are designed to provide you with unparalleled financial precision and peace of mind. I offer comprehensive bookkeeping solutions tailored to meet the unique needs of your business. From meticulous transaction recording to thorough reconciliations, I ensure that your financial records are always accurate and up-to-date.

By leveraging advanced technology and industry expertise, I deliver detailed financial insights that empower you to make informed decisions and drive your business forward. Focus on growing your business while I handle the complexities of your financial management.

Accounts Receivable

Optimize cash flow with my efficient services. I manage invoicing, follow up on outstanding amounts, and ensure timely collections. With detailed reporting and diligent oversight, I help minimize bad debts and keep your accounts in order.

Accounts Payable

Achieve seamless payment processes with thorough oversight. I handle invoice processing, payment scheduling, and reconciliation to ensure smooth operations. With precise management and detailed reporting, you’ll maintain strong vendor relationships and accurate financial records.

Payroll

I handle all aspects of payroll management, from reviewing and updating your payroll information to submitting data to your chosen payroll provider. If you don’t have a payroll company, I can assist in finding one that fits your needs and set up procedures for a smooth and reliable payroll process. Count on me to ensure your employees are paid accurately and on time.

Consulting

Small Business CFO for hire! If you don't have a full time CFO, consult with me for any of your business needs.

Finanical Analysis

Acting as your CFO on demand, I help you establish and achieve financial goals for your business over the short, medium, and long term. I start with an in-depth review of your financial situation, including revenue, cash flow, staffing efficiency, and existing procedures. Using this information, I implement financial planning strategies to guide your business towards its objectives.

We will create a budget based on our financial assessment and your goals, then utilize forecasting to track and compare actual performance against our budgeted targets.

Start-Ups

I specialize in supporting startups by ensuring the financial side of your business starts strong. I will establish efficient accounting procedures and train your employees on their roles in maintaining these processes. Additionally, I will compile reports and revenue and expense projections to keep your investors and bank informed of your business status. Consult with me for these and any other accounting challenges your startup may face.

Training

I offer comprehensive training for you and your employees on a variety of topics, including:

  • Interpreting monthly financial statements
  • Budgeting and forecasting techniques
  • Payroll entry and processing
  • Using accounting software
  • Mastering Excel spreadsheets

I also develop customized training programs tailored to your specific business needs.
